About Us

Welcome to A&A Global Dream Academy, your gateway to a world of educational opportunities. With over 25 years of industry presence and operations across India, Bangladesh, Ghana, Malaysia, Nepal, Nigeria, Pakistan, Philippines, Sri Lanka, and Vietnam, we specialize in providing comprehensive study abroad programs. Our expertise spans admission assistance to 31 countries, covering 700+ universities and 85,000+ courses. We have proudly assisted over 300,000 students in realizing their dreams of international education.

Choose A&A Global Dream Academy for personalized support, unmatched expertise, and a pathway to your dreams of studying abroad. Join us and embark on your journey to academic excellence and global success.

Our Mission:

At A&A Global Dream Academy, our mission is to empower students worldwide to achieve their dreams of international education.
